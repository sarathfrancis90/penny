What's new in 2.3.0:

• Chat history is back! Tap the menu icon or swipe from the left edge to browse past conversations.
• Conversations are grouped by date (Today, This Week, This Month, etc.) with pinned ones on top.
• Search across all your conversations from the drawer.
• Swipe a conversation left to archive it — with a quick Undo if you change your mind.
• Smarter titles: Penny now names conversations automatically after a few messages.
• Better VoiceOver support throughout the conversation list.
