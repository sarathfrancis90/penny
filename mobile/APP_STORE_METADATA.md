## Description (4000 characters max)

```
Penny is the AI-powered expense tracker built specifically for self-incorporated software professionals in Canada. Stop wrestling with spreadsheets and shoe boxes of receipts — Penny uses artificial intelligence to make expense tracking effortless, accurate, and tax-ready.

SMART AI EXPENSE ANALYSIS
Simply snap a photo of any receipt or describe an expense in plain language. Penny's AI instantly extracts the vendor, amount, date, and tax details, then automatically categorizes it using official CRA T2125 tax categories. No manual data entry. No guesswork.

CRA T2125 TAX CATEGORIES BUILT IN
Every expense is organized into the exact categories used on the CRA T2125 form for self-employed business income. From advertising and office expenses to home office deductions and vehicle costs, Penny speaks the language of Canadian tax filing. When tax season arrives, your books are already done.

CONVERSATIONAL AI ASSISTANT
Chat naturally with Penny to log expenses, ask questions about your spending, or get insights about your finances. "How much did I spend on office supplies this quarter?" — Penny knows.

COMPREHENSIVE BUDGETING
Set monthly budgets by category and track your spending in real time. Visual progress indicators show exactly where you stand. Get alerts before you overspend, not after.

GROUP EXPENSE MANAGEMENT
Running a business with partners or managing a team? Create groups to track shared expenses. Invite members, assign roles, set group budgets, and maintain a clear activity log. Perfect for small incorporated businesses with multiple directors or departments.

INCOME TRACKING
Track all revenue streams alongside your expenses for a complete financial picture. Support for recurring income, multiple frequencies, and tax categorization.

SAVINGS GOALS
Set and track savings goals with monthly contribution targets. Whether you are building an emergency fund, saving for equipment, or planning a business investment, Penny keeps you on track with visual progress and priority management.

BEAUTIFUL DASHBOARD
See your complete financial picture at a glance. Interactive charts show spending trends by category, monthly comparisons, and budget utilization. Understand where your money goes without digging through transactions.

RECEIPT MANAGEMENT
Capture receipts with your camera and attach them directly to expenses. Images are stored securely in the cloud and linked to the corresponding transaction — no more lost paper receipts.

OFFLINE CAPABLE
Log expenses even without an internet connection. Penny syncs automatically when you are back online, so you never miss recording a transaction.

PRIVACY AND SECURITY
Your financial data is protected with Firebase Authentication, comprehensive security rules, and encrypted storage. AI analysis happens server-side — your API keys and sensitive data never leave the server.

KEY FEATURES
- AI-powered receipt scanning and expense analysis
- Automatic CRA T2125 category classification
- Conversational AI assistant for natural expense logging
- Real-time budget tracking with visual progress
- Group expense management with role-based permissions
- Income tracking with recurring source support
- Savings goals with contribution tracking
- Interactive spending analytics dashboard
- Receipt photo capture and cloud storage
- Offline support with automatic sync
- Push notifications for budget alerts and group activity

Built by Canadian professionals, for Canadian professionals. Penny understands the unique needs of self-incorporated individuals navigating the Canadian tax system.
```
